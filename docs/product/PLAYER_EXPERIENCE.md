# Player Experience

## Desired feeling

- Mood: calm and beautiful
- Pace: meditative
- Difficulty: no challenge
- Pressure: none
- Aftertaste: relaxing and visually satisfying

## Player verbs

- observe
- rotate camera
- trigger fireworks
- adjust viewpoint

## Review questions

After a gameplay or visual change, answer:

- What can the player do now?
- What feels better?
- What became more confusing?
- Did the change preserve the intended mood?
- Is user playtest feedback needed?
